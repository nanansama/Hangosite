Foods & Drinks

Experience various cuisines in Egypt with your friends or on your own. You can find different restaurants all over Egypt with various aesthetics, prices, foods, and drinks.

Zooba

Learn More About Zooba!

Showcasing traditional Egyptian food at its best, Zooba is a street food staple in Cairo. Locations: Grand Museum, Zamalek, Citystars, Maadi, Downtown Mall, and more.

Cake Cafe

Learn More About Cake Cafe!

A favorite cafe since 2013, Cake Cafe serves breakfast, sandwiches, and of course, delicious desserts. Locations: Sheikh Zayed, 6 October, New Cairo, and the North Coast.

Bon Appetit

Learn More About Bon Appetit!

Established in 1986, Bon Appetit is a well-known bakery and cafe offering high-quality food at reasonable prices.

Sip

Learn More About Sip!

A premium specialty coffee shop from Lebanon, Sip offers great coffee and brunch options. Locations: Zamalek, Cinema Radio, Diplo 3, and the North Coast.
