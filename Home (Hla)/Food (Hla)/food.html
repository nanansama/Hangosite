Foods & Drinks

Experience various cuisines in Egypt with your friends or on your own. You can find different restaurants all over Egypt with various aesthetics, prices, foods and drinks.

Zooba

Learn More About Zooba!

Opting to showcase traditional Egyptian food at its best, co-founders Chris Khalifa and chef Moustafa El Refaey opened Zooba, a small and friendly joint in the centre of bustling Cairo. This is the stellar place for all-things street food. Zooba is located in various parts of Egypt: Grand Museum, Zamalek, Citystars, Maadi, Downtown Mall, Capital Promenade, Rehab, Madinaty. The food is amazing and the drinks are very unique. The vibe is absolute Awesomeness!

Cake Cafe

Learn More About Cake Cafe!

Cake Cafe has now become a Cairo phenomenon, with multiple branches in Sheikh Zayed, 6 October, New Cairo and even Hacienda on the North Coast. But it all started in Zamalek in 2013 when a tiny cafe with a few sidewalk tables opened next to Gezira Club - the start of Cake Cafe. Fast forward almost 10 years later and it’s still a Zamalek favorite for those looking for a spot to have coffee, dessert, work, or even bring their dog. Besides the cakes and desserts they’re named for, they also have breakfast, sandwiches, flatbread and pizzas & pasta.
